About NEXTOS

NEXTOS is a lightweight, fast, and secure custom ROM based on MIUI/HyperOS, focused on performance and user control. Minimal bloat, frequent updates, and a clean UI.

Highlights

Performance

Optimized for smooth responsiveness and low latency.

Privacy

Security-first approach, minimal telemetry, and timely patches.

Customization

Useful tweaks and theming without unnecessary clutter.
